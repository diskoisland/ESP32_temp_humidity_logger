Works in browsers with Web Bluetooth support, such as Chrome or Edge. Safari and Firefox generally do not support Web Bluetooth.

Logger commands

After WIFI_ON, connect your phone or laptop to the logger Wi-Fi AP and open http://192.168.4.1.

LOG_ON starts logging; the RTC must be synced and valid first (use the webpage to sync). LOG_OFF stops it. The STATUS reply shows the current logging= state.
